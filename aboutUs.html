About us

Driving school for Almere, Huizen, Blaricum, Naarden, Bussum, Hilversum, Laren, Eemnes, Baarn, Soest, Lelystad and Amsterdam.

We are the fastest growing independent driving school in Almere and the surrounding area. We provide everything you need to get you started. We take into account class times, locations and everything else you need to quickly get your driver's license at ALHAJ RIJOPLEIDING.
